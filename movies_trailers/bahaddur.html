Bahaddur(ಬಹದ್ದೂರ್):

Bahaddur
Bahaddur is a 2014 Indian Kannada-language masala film directed by Chethan Kumar and produced by R. Srinivas under R. S. Productions. The film stars Dhruva Sarja and Radhika Pandit, alongside P. Ravi Shankar, Achyuth Kumar, Jai Jagadish and Srinivasa Murthy. The music was composed by V. Harikrishna, while cinematography and editing were handled by Shreesha Kuduvalli and Deepu. S. Kumar. Bahaddur was released on 3 October 2014 in 7.1 surround sound, which is the first in Kannada cinema. The 2016 Telugu film Srirastu Subhamastu was reported to have been heavily inspired by the core plot of this film.
